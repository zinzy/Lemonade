Lemonade the corgi

Coming home on February 25th 2023

Gift tips

Some of you have expressed the desire to buy Lemonade a gift. We couldn't be more grateful!

Dog puzzle

We've heard corgis are smart dogs, and we want to keep Lemonade entertained! A fun puzzle can challenge and stimulate her problem-solving skills.

Poop bags

Literally. The more ecologically-friendly, the better.

Non-rope tug toy

We're looking forward to giving Lemonade plenty of exercise. Tug toys can do wonders for that. Rope can get stuck in her mouth, so we'd like to avoid that type of tug toy.

Chew toy

Chew toys help Lemonade's teeth and gums stay clean and healthy!

Portable feeders

We're still looking for great, portable food and drink bowls. If you know of something good, that'd make a wonderful gift.
